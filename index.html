Sayed Darwish

The Big Star of Egyptian Music

Birth

Born on March 17, 1892

In Alexandria, Egypt

Learning Music

Learned from good teachers in Alexandria

Became very good at oud and making songs

Big Works

Made more than 1,000 songs

Changed Egyptian music by mixing old and new styles

Famous Songs

"Bilady, Bilady, Bilady" - Egypt's National Song

"El Helwa Di", "Salma Ya Salama", "Zourouni"

His Impact

Called the father of Egyptian popular music

Helped many new singers in Egypt and Arab world

Death

Died on September 15, 1923

He was only 31 years old
